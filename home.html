Fresh Sushi Sushi is the most famous Japanese dish outside of Japan and one of the most popular among Japanese, who enjoy sushi on special occasions. And as such, it was evolved outside that country, influenced by different Western cultures, especially in USA. That is why many of the different types of sushi have denomination of localities of USA.

Most Famous Dishes

Salmon Roll

Salmon Roll is bursting with scrumptious flavors from buttery salmon, crab, and vegetables

Spicy Salmon

Spicy salmon roll is a type of maki made from raw salmon, spicy sauce and sushi rice, wrapped in nori

California Roll

A California roll is a maki-style sushi roll that uses avocado, cucumber, and lump crab meat instead of the traditional raw fish

Dragon Roll

Dragon roll is a tasty uramaki sushi roll featuring avocado, unagi, and shrimp tempura

Avacado roll

avocado roll is a type of sushi in which seasoned sushi rice is spread out on nori seaweed sheets, filled with avocado

Rainbow Roll

Rainbow roll is a type of uramaki sushi roll filled with cucumber, avocado and crab stick
